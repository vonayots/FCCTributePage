Time line of Dancho Kolev Danev (better known as Dan Kolov) life.

  • 1892 - born in the village of Sennik, Sevlievo
  • 1905 - left Bulgaria and went to Austro-Hungarian Empire
  • 1909 - he emigrate to the United States
  • 1914 - he started his career as a wrestler by taking part in the organised wrestling matches between the workers.
  • 1924 - wins tournament in Japan
  • 1927 - is tournament winner in Brazil
  • 1933 - for second time Diamond Belt World Heavyweight Champion
  • 1940 - Kolov died in Bulgaria from tuberculosis.
